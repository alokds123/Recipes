Chingudi Chadchadi

Description

With the famous Chilka lake and a good number of river based water resources in Odisha, prawn is a commonly available favorite for the lovers of non-vegetarian food.Here is a recipe for preparing crispy ''chadchadi' out of the prawn.

Ingridents

    250 gm tiny prawns with shell (chhota chingudi) 3 teaspoon mustard paste (sorisha, rasuna, lanka bata) (prepare by grinding mustard, garlic and red chilly) 3 green chillies (kancha lanka) Â½ teaspoon turmeric powder (haladi gunda) 2 tablespoon mustard oil (sorisha tela) Salt to taste (luna)

Steps

  1. Clean the tiny prawns but do not remove the shells
  2. Take the prawns in a kadhai or frying pan
  3. Add the mustard paste to it
  4. Also the turmeric powder, green chillies and salt
  5. Then add the mustard oil along with 1/3 cup of water
  6. mix well
  7. Put the frying pan on medium flame
  8. stir frequently
  9. Cook until it all becomes dry
  10. Serve with hot rice
